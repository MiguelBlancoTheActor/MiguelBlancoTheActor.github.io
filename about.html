Hi! I'm Miguel.

I'm a NY based aspiring actor in college for mechanical engineering.

I love to tell stories, and help others tell their story, and there’s no way more satisfying than being able to bring the characters to life.
Whether that be the main character, toiling through their hardships, or just the waiter that thanks them for dining there.
As Konstantin Stanislavski said, "There are no small parts. Only small actors."
